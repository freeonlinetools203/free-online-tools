About Loan Calculator

Our Loan Calculator is a free online tool that helps you calculate total loan cost, including total interest and overall payment amount. Enter your loan details to instantly see how much interest you will pay over time.

How to Use This Tool

  • Enter the Loan Amount
  • Enter the Interest Rate (%)
  • Enter the Loan Duration
  • Click on "Calculate"
  • See total interest and total payment instantly
