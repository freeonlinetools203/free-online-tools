How QR Codes Work

QR (Quick Response) codes store information in a matrix of black and white squares. When scanned with a smartphone camera, the code is decoded to reveal the information. QR codes can store up to 4,296 alphanumeric characters, making them perfect for URLs, contact details, WiFi credentials, and more. Our QR code maker generates high-resolution codes that work with any modern smartphone.
