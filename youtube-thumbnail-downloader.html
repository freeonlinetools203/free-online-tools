YouTube Thumbnail Sizes and Specifications

Understanding YouTube thumbnail specifications is important for creators. YouTube recommends using thumbnails with a resolution of 1280×720 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio) with a minimum width of 640 pixels. The file size should be under 2MB, and common formats are JPG, GIF, BMP, or PNG.

How YouTube Thumbnails Work

YouTube automatically generates thumbnails for every video using frames from the video. However, creators can upload custom thumbnails for better click-through rates. YouTube stores these thumbnails at different resolutions on their servers. Our youtube thumbnails downloader tool accesses these server URLs to fetch the original images.

The highest quality thumbnail (maxresdefault.jpg) is only available for videos that have HD/4K enabled and have a custom thumbnail uploaded. If a video doesn't have a custom thumbnail or HD quality, our tool automatically falls back to the next available quality.
