🔒 Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Free Online Tools, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our free online calculators and utilities. We believe in complete transparency and want you to feel safe while using our tools.

📊 What Information Do We Collect?

We do NOT collect any personal information. All our tools run entirely in your browser. We do not:

  • ❌ Store your text, numbers, or calculations
  • ❌ Track your activity or browsing behavior
  • ❌ Use cookies to identify you
  • ❌ Share any data with third parties
  • ❌ Require registration or login

🛠️ How Our Tools Work

All calculations and text processing happen locally in your browser. When you use our age calculator, BMI calculator, word counter, or any other tool, your data never leaves your device. This means:

  • ✅ Your information stays private
  • ✅ No servers store your data
  • ✅ You can use tools offline after loading
  • ✅ Complete anonymity guaranteed

We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics. Some essential technical cookies may be used for basic functionality (like remembering your dark mode preference), but these do not collect personal information. No third-party tracking scripts are used on our website.

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our tools are designed for general audiences and do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. All tools are safe for users of all ages.

🌍 Data Security

Since we don't store any data on our servers, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information. Your privacy is protected by design.
