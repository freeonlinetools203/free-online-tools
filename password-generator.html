🧠 What Is a Password Generator?

A Password Generator is an online tool that automatically creates strong passwords using a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols. ✅ What Makes a Password Secure?

A secure password should:

  • Be at least 12–16 characters long
  • Include both uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Contain numbers
  • Include special symbols
  • Avoid personal information
  • Not contain common dictionary words

Example of weak password: mypassword123
Example of strong password: R7@kL9!pX2#tQ4

🔎 Why Use a Random Password Generator Instead of Creating Your Own?

Humans naturally create memorable passwords, often including names, dates, or patterns. Hackers are aware of these habits. A Strong Password Generator Online removes predictability and creates complex combinations that are extremely difficult to guess.

  • Never share passwords publicly
