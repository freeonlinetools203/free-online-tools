EMI Calculator

What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month towards your loan. It includes both principal and interest components.

How EMI is Calculated?

EMI is calculated using the formula: EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N – 1] Where P = Loan Amount, R = Monthly Interest Rate, N = Number of Months.
