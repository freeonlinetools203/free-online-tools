🔵 What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly payment made by a borrower to repay a loan. It includes both principal and interest amount.

🔵 EMI Formula

EMI = (P × R × (1+R)^N) / ((1+R)^N - 1)
