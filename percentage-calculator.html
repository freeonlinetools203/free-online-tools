🔵 Example Calculations

Example 1: Discount Calculator

If a product costs ₹500 and you want to apply a 20% discount, enter:

  • Percentage = 20
  • Total Value = 500

Result: ₹100 discount → New price = ₹400

Example 2: Exam Marks Percentage

If you scored 450 marks out of 600, calculate your percentage:

Percentage = (450 ÷ 600) × 100 = 75%

Example 3: Profit and Loss Percentage

If you bought a product for ₹200 and sold it for ₹250:

Profit % = ((250 - 200) ÷ 200) × 100 = 25%
