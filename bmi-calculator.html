BMI Calculator

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. It helps determine whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

How to Use This BMI Calculator

Enter your weight in kilograms and height in centimeters. Click on Calculate BMI to instantly get your result.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater
